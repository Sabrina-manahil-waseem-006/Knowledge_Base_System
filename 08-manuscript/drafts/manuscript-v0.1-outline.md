## Title
Scalable Rule-Based Inference Architecture (SRBIA): A Systematic Literature Review of Modular Designs, Algorithms, and Performance Optimization (1978-2024)

## Abstract (Structured)
- **Background:** Addressing the "Rule Explosion" problem in large-scale KBS.
- **Objectives:** Identifying scalable patterns and inference bottlenecks (4 RQs).
- **Methods:** PRISMA 2020 protocol, Google Scholar/Semantic Scholar search (n=500), dual-reviewer screening.
- **Results:** Synthesis of 12 high-impact studies; identification of modular partitioning as a key scalability driver.
- **Conclusions:** Proposed SRBIA framework for future-ready inference engines.
