# Decision Log — SRBIA Review

Append-only record of all methodological decisions for transparency per PRISMA 2020 item 24c.

---

## 2026-01-15 — Topic Scope Definition
**Decision:** Limit scope to "Scalable Rule-Based Inference Architecture (SRBIA)". Exclude simple expert systems without performance data and exclude generic AI papers that don't focus on rule matching algorithms.
**Rationale:** "Rule-based" is a broad term. Narrowing to architectural scalability (like Rete/TREAT optimizations) makes the review technically deep.

---

## 2026-02-01 — Date Range
**Decision:** Search from 1978 to 2024.
**Rationale:** 1978 marks the beginning of major production system research (Forgy’s early work). 2024 captures modern hybrid rule engines.

---

## 2026-02-18 — Publication Quality
**Decision:** Include peer-reviewed journals (Elsevier, IEEE) and conference papers. Exclude non-peer-reviewed blog posts or industry whitepapers.
**Rationale:** To maintain high methodological rigor for the KBS course project.

---

## 2026-04-08 — Screening Tool
**Decision:** Use Excel with dual-independent sheets.
**Rationale:** Managed by R1 (SE-23006) and R2 (SE-23003) for transparency in conflict resolution.
