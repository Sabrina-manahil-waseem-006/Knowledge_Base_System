# Team Roles and Responsibilities

## Review Team

| Role | Name | Affiliation | Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|---|
| Guarantor / Senior author | Dr. A. Researcher | University NLP Lab | Overall direction, final decisions, manuscript guarantor |
| Reviewer 1 (R1) | Graduate Student 1 | University NLP Lab | Independent screening, extraction, risk of bias |
| Reviewer 2 (R2) | Graduate Student 2 | University NLP Lab | Independent screening, extraction, risk of bias |
| Information Specialist | Dr. B. Methods | University Library | Search strategy design and peer review |
| Statistical Advisor | Dr. C. Stats | Statistics Department | Synthesis approach guidance |

## Decision Authority

- **Inclusion/exclusion conflicts**: Discussed by R1 and R2; unresolved
  cases adjudicated by the guarantor.
- **Protocol amendments**: Require guarantor approval, dated entry in the
  decision log, and an updated protocol registered on OSF.
- **Risk of bias disagreements**: Resolved through discussion; persistent
  disagreements escalated to a methodologist.

## PRISMA 2020 alignment

This review follows PRISMA 2020 (Page et al., BMJ 2021) for reporting and
PRISMA-P 2015 (Moher et al., Syst Rev 2015) for the protocol.

## Communication

- Bi-weekly team meeting: alternate Wednesdays 14:00
- Shared workspace: this Git repository
- Issue tracking: GitHub Issues
